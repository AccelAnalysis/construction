Service Pillar

Builder & Developer Radar™

Instead of a generic list, you get a ranked “hit list” of builders, developers, and GCs that can realistically feed your business for the next 3–5 years.

  • Ideal Buyer Profile: We define your sweet spot by trade, project size, scope, and radius.
  • Market Scan: We map builders, developers, and GCs that match your criteria.
  • Fit Scoring: Each record is tagged by project types, contract value, geography, and work mix.
  • Priority Tiers: Your top 25 “anchor” accounts, next 50 “growth” accounts, and a strategic watch list.

Deliverable: a living radar — spreadsheet plus map layer — that your team can use for business development, preconstruction strategy, and long-term relationship building.

Service Pillar

GC–Sub Matchmaking™

We help GCs build stronger vendor benches and help subs get onto more of the right bid lists — using structured criteria and warm introductions.

  • GC Inputs: project types, average project size, geography, required trades, insurance and safety thresholds.
  • Sub Inputs: primary trades, typical contract size, travel radius, crew capacity, certifications, and past project highlights.
  • Matching Engine: we score fits based on trade, geography, project size, and strategic goals (including diversity or local participation).
  • Intro Campaigns: we run targeted, invitation-style outreach to fill pre-qual forms and schedule short intro conversations.

Result: fewer “hail-Mary” bids, more repeatable relationships, and vendor benches that are built intentionally — not pieced together under deadline pressure.
